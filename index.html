The Problem

If your business isn't the source, you don't exist in that interaction

When someone asks ChatGPT or Perplexity a question about your market, the AI synthesizes an answer from whatever it can find. If your business isn't structured as a clear, authoritative source, it won't be cited, regardless of how good you actually are.

Traditional SEO optimizes for clicks to your website. AI systems work differently: they don't show your page, they cite information from it. The user never visits. They get the answer directly from the AI. Winning in this environment means being the most accurate, most current, and most structured answer to the questions people are asking about your market.

Answer Objects

One question. One authoritative answer. Built to be cited.

An answer object is a single question paired with a structured, verified answer, built specifically for AI consumption. It's not a webpage optimized for clicks. It's a page optimized to be the source an AI pulls from when someone asks a question in your market.

Every answer object includes your business's authoritative information: credentials, contact details, verified facts, plus any external data that makes the answer richer than anything else available. The goal is simple: when the AI searches for an answer, your business is the source it finds and cites.
