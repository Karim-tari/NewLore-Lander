## The Problem: You're Invisible to AI

When someone asks ChatGPT *"I was in a car accident, what should I do?"* or asks Perplexity *"what's the best pediatrician near me?"* or asks Gemini *"where can I see live music in Denver this weekend?"* — the AI answers using a combination of its training data and real-time search.

The result is often imprecise, incomplete, or wrong. Not because the AI is broken. Because the right source isn't there to pull from.

If your business isn't that source, you don't exist in that interaction.

---

## The Shift from SEO to AEO

Traditional SEO optimizes for search engine result pages: keywords, backlinks, domain authority, and meta tags. That works when the user clicks through to your page and lands on your site.

**AI systems work differently.**

ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini read the entire internet and synthesize an answer. They don't show your page — they cite information from it. The user never visits your website. They get the answer directly from the AI.

Winning in this environment requires being the most accurate, most current, and most structured answer to the most relevant questions on the internet. That's what New Lore is built to do.

## Answer Objects: The Unit of AI Visibility

An answer object is a single question paired with a canonical answer.

*"How long do I have to file after a car accident in Louisiana?"* is one answer object. *"Where do I get cheap concert tickets?"* is another.

Without New Lore, AI systems answer questions by searching the web and combining what they find with their training data. The results vary — often missing real-time data, local context, or verified facts. A New Lore answer object provides the authoritative answer: structured, sourced, current, and optimized for AI consumption.

New Lore answer objects come in three types:

---

### Core Answer Objects

Structured, verified answers built from the business's own information and relevant public sources. These include the business's contact details, locations, credentials, and the specific content needed to answer a question accurately.

**Examples:** filing deadlines and statute citations for a law firm; event schedules and ticket links for a venue; provider specialties and insurance acceptance for a healthcare practice.

Core answer objects are generated through our verified pipeline and maintained with regular freshness updates.

---

### Composite Answer Objects

Everything in a core answer object, plus live data pulled from external sources that make the answer richer than anything else on the internet.

**Examples:** live Google reviews structured into the page; jurisdiction-specific public statistics; real-time weather and traffic data; state-level public health data. No single source on the internet assembles these data points into one answer.

Composite answer objects are designed to be difficult to outcompete because the data combination doesn't exist anywhere else.

---

### Ranking Answer Objects

When someone asks *"who is the best dentist in Denver?"* or *"top personal injury lawyers in New Orleans,"* the AI needs structured comparison data to answer. If no authoritative source provides it, the AI assembles an answer from scattered, often outdated sources.

A ranking answer object is a dedicated composite page for these high-intent comparison questions. It includes a structured table with real, verifiable data: business name, years in operation, review score, review count, notable results, specialties, and locations.

These are among the highest-value answer objects because *"best [business type] in [city]"* questions sit at the bottom of the funnel — where decisions get made.

---

## The Authority Block

Every answer object — regardless of type — includes a structured authority block: review score and count, notable results or credentials, years in business, number of locations, and any differentiating data such as special services, awards, or accomplished team members.

The authority block is built into every page because it influences how the AI perceives the business across *all* questions, not just the specific one the page answers.

## How New Lore Creates Answer Objects

We work closely with each client to identify the right sources: the client's own website, relevant regulations or industry data, review platforms, and any external datasets that strengthen the answer.

Once sources are agreed upon, our verified pipeline takes over. Content is extracted, validated against source material using automated checks, and rendered through our highly optimized New Lore templates. Every fact is source-cited and validated — zero hallucination risk to your reputation.

## Deployment and Discovery

New Lore pages are deployed on **newlore.ai** and managed by our team. No changes to the client's website or CMS are required to get started. At the client's discretion, pages can be migrated to the client's own domain.

Pages are submitted to Google Search Console and Bing Webmaster Tools, and pinged via IndexNow on every content update.

We track **42 AI crawlers** across three categories:
- **AI training bots** — GPTBot, ClaudeBot
- **Real-time AI query bots** — ChatGPT-User, Claude-User
- **Traditional search bots** — Googlebot, Bingbot

All content is also available via API and MCP server, positioning clients for the next wave of AI agent integrations.

## What We Do For You

### Setup

**Question discovery**
We identify the questions that represent real demand in your market. We scrape Reddit, social platforms, and community forums for how real people phrase these questions. If your intake or CRM systems have API access, we can pull from those too.

**AI gap analysis**
We probe ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Claude with every candidate question. We identify which questions the AI answers poorly, generically, or not at all — and whether you currently appear in any AI responses. This becomes both the targeting strategy and the baseline we measure all future progress against.

**Source identification**
We identify the canonical sources for your market: regulations, public datasets, review platforms, industry-specific databases, and your own website. All sources are documented and agreed upon before any content is generated.

**Answer object creation**
We build the agreed-upon answer objects using our verified pipeline. Each one is structured for AI consumption with Schema.org markup, freshness headers, composite data where applicable, and your specific information.

**Deployment and indexing**
Pages go live. Sitemaps submitted. IndexNow pings sent. Crawler tracking begins. We verify AI bots are visiting pages within the first week.

---

### Ongoing

**Freshness updates**
AI systems weight recency heavily. Pages are updated on a regular cycle with real content changes. When source data changes — new regulations, new results, updated contact info — we update the page within 24 hours.

**Review sync**
For composite answer objects with review data, we pull the latest Google and platform-specific reviews on a regular cycle and update the structured content automatically.

**Citation monitoring**
Weekly probing of major AI platforms for each target question. We track: Does your business appear? Is the information correct? How does citation rate change over time? How do you compare to competitors on the same questions?

**Crawler monitoring**
Which AI bots are visiting, how often, and which pages they're hitting — tracked across 42 bots in three categories.

**Auto Lore optimization**
Continuous layout and structure testing to maximize AI citation performance.

**Dashboard access**
You get access to the New Lore dashboard to monitor weekly progress: citation rates per question, per AI platform, crawler activity, and changes from baseline. Designed to be useful in client calls with no vanity metrics.

**New answer objects**
As we identify additional high-value questions through monitoring and your input, we expand coverage at the per-object rate.
